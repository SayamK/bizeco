About us

Proud of its logo and the tagline “Mind your own business!”, this club hosted its premiere inter school competition last year in the month of October. The Event was a huge success with 9 teams from the best schools in New Delhi participating in it. BIZECO shows its ambition for business & economics and thus gets the name “BIZ” from business and “ECO” from economics. The main aim of this club is to give the students exposure of commercial aspects of life and providing them with the knowledge of the modern day world. BIZECO also inculcates values and ethical ways of conducting business among the students via its multiple events and simulators. Known for its amazing ideas and advanced technology used in the events, it also boasts over its “paperless fest” scheme! The club possesses the finest events any commerce club may have in the capital ranging from stock market evaluation and property trading to entrepreneurship and the barter system! As the technology gets advanced day by day, BIZECO adapts to the new technology to make its events more futuristic.
